  • Requirements

    Municipality own more then 50% of building, taking care of them and collect rent every month that goes back to people living there in form of reconstructions and building new apartments.

    In order to apply for one apartment, you have to:

    1. Have valid ID card showing address in a city
    2. You or your family member have to work/study or have some income

    Additional requirements for non-citizens

    • Have lived in a city for 5 years
    • Hold permanent residence permit
    • Speak one of official languages of a country
    • Have clean criminal record from land you are citizen in for the last 10 years

    Whether your request for a housing benefit will be approved and when depends on the following factors:

    • Size of the flat
    • Size of your family
    • Which part of the city you wish to live in

    You have to remember that the waiting lists are long and that we are not able to offer everyone an apartment. Our wish is for all citizens to get use of their right to municipal housing.
